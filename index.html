Universidad Europea Madrid

MASTER’S DEGREE IN ARCHITECTURE



The Degree in Architecture at Universidad Europea in Madrid is a five-year programme aimed at providing students with the knowledge and skills to lead architecture projects around the world. The architecture degree, which totals 300 ECTS, ensures you will graduate having knowledge of the latest digital tools in the industry, such as BIM Methodology, Drawing, Projects and Urbanism, among others
